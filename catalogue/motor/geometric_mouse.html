geometric_mouse is a parasitic bug that taps on things. It uses fled circuit to drive a tiny pager motor. It has a long flexible lead on the tapper so you can form the wire to situate the motor onto desirable place or thing. If you put them leaning on a glass window it will click and tap the window. If you put them gently touching a plant it will wiggle the plant.
