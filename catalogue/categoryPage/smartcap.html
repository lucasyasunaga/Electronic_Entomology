smartcap

Smartcap group possesses a circuit based on a solar engine called "smartcap" that is based on a design by Bob Shannon that can be read here under BEAM Circuits >> nocturnal. This group is sensitive to a loss of light and they give a bursts when they see the dark. Some can cry in slightest change of daylight as well, crying when the cloud covers the sun.
